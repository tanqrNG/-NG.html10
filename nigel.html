Advantages of the ICT Strand Enrolling in the ICT strand for SHS gives senior high school graduates valuable knowledge that enables them to either dive immediately into technical professions befitting their interests and specializations, or to pursue further studies aligned with their track.

ICT is a Senior High School strand that prepares you for a career in Information and Communication Technology. It equips you with advanced skills in computer systems, programming, creating web pages, and basic animation. What you will gain from the ICT Strand: Learn about programming, animation or illustration.

As ICT in education advances informatization, not only teachers who support learning but also students will be able to acquire knowledge and skills of IT devices. literacy will help students to select and use necessary information and to develop the ability to think for themselves.

Students choose the ICT strand over other strands due to several factors. One of the main reasons is the students' interest and hobby in computers, programming, and technology, which motivates them to pursue ICT studies.
